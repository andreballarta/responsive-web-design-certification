Scrambled Eggs

Fluffy and creamy eggs ready in just 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper

Instructions

  1. Gently whisk the eggs in a bowl with the salt and pepper.
  2. Melt all the butter in a pan over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and stir gently until they are soft.
  4. Remove the pan from the heat while the eggs are still slightly wet.
  5. Optional: serve them with toast.
